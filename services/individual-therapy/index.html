Individual Therapy

One-on-one psychological support tailored to your needs.

A Safe Space to Talk

Individual therapy provides a confidential, supportive environment where you can explore your thoughts, feelings, and behaviours with a trained professional. Whether you're dealing with a specific issue or seeking personal growth, therapy offers the tools and space to make meaningful changes.

I work with adults and young adults on a wide range of concerns, including:

  • Anxiety and worry
  • Depression and low mood
  • Stress and burnout
  • Self-esteem and confidence
  • Life transitions and adjustment
  • Grief and loss
  • Anger management
  • Sleep difficulties
  • Health anxiety
  • Work-life balance

What to Expect

In our first session, we'll discuss what brings you in, your goals, and your history. Together, we'll develop a tailored treatment plan using evidence-based approaches such as CBT, ACT, DBT, and Schema Therapy.

Sessions are typically 50 minutes and can be held weekly or fortnightly depending on your needs. I aim to create a warm, non-judgmental space where you feel comfortable exploring difficult topics at your own pace.

I also offer telehealth sessions for clients who prefer to meet online.
