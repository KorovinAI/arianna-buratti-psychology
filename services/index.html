How I Can Help

I offer a range of evidence-based psychological services. Each session is tailored to your individual needs, goals, and circumstances.

Individual Therapy

One-on-one support for adults and young adults navigating anxiety, depression, stress, self-esteem, and life transitions.

Relationship Work

Helping individuals understand and improve their relationship patterns, navigate conflict, and build healthier connections.

ADHD

Counselling and support for adults and children living with attention deficit and hyperactivity-related challenges.

Anxiety & Depression

Effective strategies for managing anxiety, low mood, panic attacks, and building long-term emotional wellbeing.

Personality Disorders

Support, counselling, and screening assessments for individuals experiencing personality disorder-related challenges.

Telehealth Available

All services are also available via secure video sessions for clients across Australia.
