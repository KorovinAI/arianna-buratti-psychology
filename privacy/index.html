Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Overview

Arianna Buratti Psychology is committed to protecting your privacy and handling your personal information responsibly. This policy explains how I collect, use, store, and disclose your personal information in accordance with the Australian Privacy Act 1988 and the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs).

Information I Collect

I may collect the following types of personal information:

  • Name, contact details (phone, email, address)
  • Date of birth and Medicare details
  • Referral information from your GP or other health professionals
  • Health information relevant to your psychological care
  • Session notes and treatment plans
  • Information you provide via the website contact form

How I Use Your Information

Your personal information is used to:

  • Provide psychological assessment and treatment
  • Communicate with you about appointments and care
  • Process Medicare claims and invoicing
  • Comply with legal and regulatory obligations
  • Respond to enquiries submitted via the website

Disclosure

I will not disclose your personal or health information to third parties without your consent, except where:

  • Required or authorised by law (e.g., mandatory reporting obligations)
  • Necessary to prevent a serious threat to life, health, or safety
  • You have provided written consent for disclosure to a specific party (e.g., your GP)

Storage & Security

Your records are stored securely in accordance with AHPRA and APS guidelines. Electronic records are protected by encryption and access controls. Paper records, where applicable, are stored in locked facilities.

Client records are retained for a minimum of seven years after the last session, or until a minor client turns 25, in accordance with Australian legal requirements.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information I hold about you
  • Request corrections to inaccurate information
  • Make a complaint about how your information has been handled

To make a request or complaint, please contact me at info@ab-psych.com.au or call 0490 786 047.

Website

This website does not use cookies or third-party tracking. The contact form sends your message directly to my email via a third-party form service (Formspree). Your submission data is not stored on this website.

Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.

Compassionate, evidence-based psychological care to help individuals navigate life's challenges and achieve lasting wellbeing.
