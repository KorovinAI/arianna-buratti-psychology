About Arianna

Registered Psychologist in Carlton, Melbourne

Arianna Buratti

Registered Psychologist • MAPS • AHPRA

I'm a registered psychologist based in Carlton, Melbourne, with years of experience in the mental health space. I bring a deep understanding of the complexities of human behaviour across various cultural and socioeconomic backgrounds.

My commitment to inclusivity and tailored therapeutic approaches means that every client receives care that is personalised to their unique needs, goals, and circumstances.

I work with individuals and families, with a focus on relationship dynamics, personality challenges, ADHD, anxiety, and depression. I also offer sessions in Italian for clients who prefer it.

I'm registered with AHPRA (Australian Health Practitioner Regulation Agency) and a proud member of the Australian Psychological Society (APS).

My Therapeutic Approach

I draw from a range of evidence-based approaches, tailoring each session to what works best for you.

Cognitive Behavioural Therapy (CBT)

Identifying and changing unhelpful thought patterns and behaviours.

Dialectical Behaviour Therapy (DBT)

Building skills for emotional regulation, distress tolerance, and mindfulness.

Acceptance & Commitment Therapy (ACT)

Developing psychological flexibility and values-based living.

Schema Therapy

Addressing deep-rooted patterns and unmet emotional needs.

Trauma-Informed Practice

Safe, sensitive approaches to processing trauma and building resilience.

Solution-Focused Brief Therapy

Goal-oriented strategies for creating positive change efficiently.

Interpersonal Therapy (IPT)

Improving communication and relationship patterns to ease emotional distress.

Mindfulness-Based Cognitive Therapy

Combining cognitive therapy with mindfulness techniques to prevent relapse.
