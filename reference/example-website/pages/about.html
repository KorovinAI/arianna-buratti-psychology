Liela Vergara | Registered Psychologist and Principal of Cognito Psychology

Master of Professional Psychology, Graduate Diploma in Psychology, Bachelor of Arts (Media & Communications) in Political Economy and Communications.

Liela aims to assist clients in navigating psychological challenges within a supportive therapeutic environment. She prioritises building therapeutic relationships through a collaborative approach, aiming to create a safe, understanding, and validating space. Within this environment, she works with each client to help identify their goals and develop practical strategies, while providing attentive listening grounded in ethical practice. She emphasises the importance of acknowledging progress throughout therapy, which may support a sense of confidence, self-belief, and hope.

Liela Vergara is a Registered Psychologist at Cognito Psychology. Her professional experience encompasses private practice and leadership, including a role as a Regional Manager where she provided professional oversight and guidance to a team of psychologists. Prior to her career in psychology, she worked in the Australian dental health sector.

Liela supports individuals experiencing a range of psychological challenges using therapies informed by current evidence and professional training. Her approach includes Cognitive Behavioural Therapy (CBT), Schema Therapy, and Trauma-Focused CBT. When supporting couples to explore helpful changes in their relationships, she utilises the Gottman Method in her work, having completed Level 1 Training in this framework. She also offers interventions to assist clients when working towards employment goals and has experience working with individuals within the disability sector.

Liela holds a Master of Professional Psychology, a Graduate Diploma in Psychology, and a Bachelor’s degree. She completed her undergraduate qualifications at the University of New South Wales and her postgraduate qualifications at the University of New England and Western Sydney University. She actively engages in ongoing professional development to stay current with therapeutic practices.

Outside of her professional work, Liela enjoys exploring different cuisines, connecting with nature, and spending time with her dogs, cat, and loved ones.

Liela is SIRA-approved to deliver psychological services for NSW WorkCover and CTP (Compulsory Third Party) insurance claims. Registration number: PSY0002103395.

Our Vision 

Supporting Mental Wellbeing and Individual Growth

At Cognito Psychology in Ultimo, we are committed to supporting mental wellbeing and individual growth. We acknowledge the potential for change and development within individuals. We aim to provide a supportive and professional environment where clients can explore their thoughts and feelings in a respectful and non-judgemental manner.

Through a collaborative approach, we tailor our support to your needs to help you develop greater self-awareness and self-acceptance. We aim to support personal growth and resilience using evidence-informed approaches.

We recognise the complex interplay of biological, psychological, and social factors that influence individual wellbeing. We aim to support clients in enhancing their overall wellbeing, addressing mental, emotional, and social aspects. We acknowledge the individual’s experience of spiritual and physical health, and will collaborate with other health professionals as required.

We are dedicated to contributing positively to the wellbeing of our clients and the community. We value diversity and strive to provide culturally sensitive and inclusive care that respects the unique experiences and identities of all individuals.

Our approach is to help you work towards your goals, one step at a time.
