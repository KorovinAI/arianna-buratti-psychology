Psychological Services for Individuals in Ultimo, Sydney

Cognito Psychology: Professional Psychological Services

Evidence-Informed, Collaborative Care

At Cognito Psychology, we offer a range of evidence-informed psychological services in Ultimo, Sydney NSW. Our approach integrates current research and professional training to support each client’s needs. We believe in building strong therapeutic relationships, creating a safe and supportive space for you to explore your thoughts and feelings.

Our Psychology Services for Individuals

Couples Therapy 

We offer support for couples seeking to manage relationship challenges

Relationship Counselling 

Exploring strategies aimed at addressing communication, managing conflict, and exploring healthier connections.

Anxiety and Mood-Related Concerns 

 We provide therapeutic support for individuals experiencing anxiety, low mood, and other mood-related challenges, using evidence-informed approaches.

Support for Managing Anger 

Focusing on the development of skills aimed at managing anger and exploring strategies to cope with stress.

Obsessions and Compulsions

Exploring therapeutic strategies aimed at addressing obsessive thoughts and compulsive behaviours.

Adjustment and Life Transitions

 Therapy for those experiencing major life shifts, including grief, divorce, and workplace change.

Stress, Burnout, and Work-Life Balance

Focusing on the development of skills aimed at managing work-related stress, navigating work-life balance, and exploring strategies to address burnout.

Trauma Therapy 

Support for individuals aimed at processing traumatic experiences and exploring coping strategies.

Psychological Support for Young Adults and Students (18+)

Support for young people navigating challenges such as anxiety, low mood, and relationship issues.

LGBTQIA+ Mental Health Support

Supporting LGBTQIA+ individuals in addressing mental health concerns within an inclusive framework.

Therapy for Older Adults 

We offer a calm and understanding space for older adults to discuss their concerns and goals.

Social Anxiety 

Exploring cognitive patterns and developing strategies that may help to manage social fears.

WorkCover and CTP (SIRA) 

Therapy aimed at supporting your wellbeing and return-to-work journey after a workplace injury or motor vehicle accident.

Eating and Body Image Concerns  

Therapeutic support for individuals navigating eating and body image concerns, using evidence-informed approaches.

ADHD 

Therapy for individuals with ADHD to explore strategies that may help with day-to-day challenges.

A Comfortable Space for Older Adults

We understand that older adults may require a quieter and more peaceful environment to discuss their concerns. Our practice offers a relaxing setting where you can feel comfortable and at ease.

Language Services – Spanish Speaking Psychologist in Ultimo, Sydney

We offer services in both English and Spanish. Our bilingual registered psychologist, Liela Vergara, is fluent in Spanish and provides culturally sensitive care to Spanish-speaking clients.

If you are seeking professional support, please contact our practice to discuss an appointment.
