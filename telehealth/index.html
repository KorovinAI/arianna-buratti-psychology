Telehealth

Secure video sessions from the comfort of your home.

Therapy From Anywhere in Australia

All of my services are available via secure video call, making it easy to access psychological support regardless of where you are in Australia. Telehealth sessions follow the same structure, duration, and evidence-based approach as in-person sessions.

Who Is Telehealth For?

Telehealth may be a good fit if you:

  • Live outside Melbourne or in a regional area
  • Have mobility or health challenges that make travel difficult
  • Prefer the comfort and privacy of your own space
  • Have a busy schedule and want to reduce travel time
  • Are already an existing client and want flexibility

How It Works

1

Book a Session

Get in touch to schedule a telehealth appointment. Mention that you'd prefer video when booking.

2

Receive Your Link

Before your session, you'll receive a secure video link via email. No software installation needed.

3

Join Your Session

Click the link at your appointment time. All you need is a device with a camera, microphone, and a quiet space.

Fees & Medicare

Telehealth sessions are the same fee as in-person sessions ($200). Medicare rebates apply in the same way — you'll need a valid Mental Health Treatment Plan and GP referral.

For full details on fees and how to access Medicare rebates, see the Fees & Rebates page.
